Welcome to Gino's Restaurant

We specialise in food that is quick and tasty and we pride ourselves on our famous quality. Take a look at our menu, we have a great range of entrees, mains, deserts or something for the kids.

Open 7 days a week, with late trading Thursday through Sunday. You can find our hours on the Location and Trading Hours page.

We have professional staff working to provide you with the best food in Melbourne. The team is led by our head chef Gino who has been a 2 star chef for the last 10 years with 18 years experience as a chef.

Try a taste of Italy, Greece or Turkey with many traditional recipes to make your mouth water
